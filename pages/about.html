Under the hood

Detection technology

Three forensic layers work in parallel to classify every communication with speed and precision.

Layer 01
Signal dictionary
70+ curated keyword signals across three categories: high-risk (fraud, cover-up), medium-risk (policy violations), and safe (compliance indicators) — with weighted scoring that rewards safe language and penalizes misconduct signals.
Layer 02
Pattern engines
Five contextual regex engines that detect multi-word fraud constructs: financial fraud, data exfiltration, urgency manipulation, threatening language, and identity deception — signals that single-keyword matching always misses.
Layer 03
Behavioral heuristics
Statistical analysis of message structure: stacked urgency signals, abnormally short and aggressive messages, and signal density ratios — adding context that pure pattern matching cannot capture.
Output
Case reports
Structured JSON results carrying verdict, risk tier, confidence (0–100%), raw score (/20), full evidence array with signal type and level, word count, and ISO timestamp — ready for downstream integration.
Next
LLM integration
Phase 1 roadmap: the rule-based scoring engine is replaced by a fine-tuned language model. Output shape is identical — making the transition seamless for any downstream consumer of the API.
Deployment
Zero infrastructure
The current prototype runs entirely in the browser — no server, no database, no build tool. Open index.html and the full engine is live. Designed for rapid pilot demonstration to any stakeholder.
