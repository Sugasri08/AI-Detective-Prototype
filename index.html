The investigation process

How the detective
finds the truth

Three layers of forensic analysis work together — from raw pattern matching to contextual intelligence — to surface what matters.

01 — Ingest
Evidence submission
Paste any written communication — email, memo, chat log, ticket — into the analyzer. The engine accepts raw text of any length and begins processing immediately.
02 — Scan
Forensic signal detection
Three parallel scans run simultaneously: a keyword dictionary (70+ signals), five contextual regex pattern engines, and behavioral heuristics that catch stacking manipulation tactics.
03 — Report
Case file generation
Every finding produces a structured case report: a unique ID, confidence score, highlighted transcript with color-coded evidence, and a grouped evidence log — ready for escalation or audit.
Capabilities

Built for investigators,
compliance teams, and analysts

Three-tier risk classification
Every communication is classified as Suspicious, Needs Review, or Safe — with a confidence percentage and an internal risk score that drives the classification, not a black box.
Explainable evidence log
Every verdict is backed by a cited evidence log. Risk signals are highlighted inline in the source text — analysts see exactly what triggered the alert and why, not just a score.
Contextual pattern matching
Five regex-based pattern engines catch multi-word fraud signals that keyword lists miss: financial fraud, data exfiltration, urgency manipulation, threat language, and identity deception.
Risk confidence meter
An animated confidence bar translates the raw risk score into a 0–100% confidence percentage, giving analysts a fast intuitive read on severity without needing to interpret raw numbers.
Unique case IDs & timestamps
Every analysis generates a unique case reference (AID-YEAR-XXXXX) with a precise timestamp — creating an immutable, auditable investigation record for legal, HR, and compliance use.
Zero-infrastructure deployment
The prototype runs entirely in the browser — no server, no database, no installation. Open index.html and the full forensic engine is live. Designed for rapid demonstration and pilot rollout.
